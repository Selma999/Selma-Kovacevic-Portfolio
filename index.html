About

My name is Selma Kovacevic. I was born in Heilbronn, Germany. After moving back to Bosnia and Herezegovina, I started elementary school in a small, yet breathtaking town, in the north of B&H, Bosanska Krupa. I graduated from Gymnasium. I have earned my Bachelor's in Physics at Faculty of Science, University of Sarajevo. My love for physics started back in childhood, but while studying I realized how much fun I had with programming and how everything depends on it nowadays. Moreover, aside of my diploma, physics gave me much more, for one, the love and passion for programming. I have to admit that I've always had a passion for design, so after realizing that as a developer I have the chance to combine engineering, physics and design, I've decided to change my career path. Programming gives me the best possible combination of all of my desired career fields, thus I can say for myself I'm an aspiring software developer.
